SEP Course

SemeseterEvaluationProject

The sep it’s a group project that tries to encompass all the knowledge acquired during the semester. It’s usually made in groups of 4 or more and it’s worth 15 credits witch means it’s is the most important course of the semester.

Before getting stared with the group project the class had lectures on how to handle conflict, the different levels a conflict could reach and how to try to deescalate the situation. Also the class is given models on how to work together as a group leaving the decision on witch model to use to the individuals, also briefed on group mechanics and different stages when working in a group.

Even while being a group project the SEP requires every member of the group to dedicate an individual level of effort, no social loafing (to know more follow:the link). Since the final evaluation is individual it’s possible for a student to have a failing grade even with a perfectly good project.

For this semester we the groups are required to create an application that creates an usable weekly schedule of the course with the possibility to change it at the admin’s will. This should be displayed in an web page. The groups were given specific guidelines to keep in mind when designing the system for example the classes shouldn’t exceed 45 students, be able to book rooms and more.
